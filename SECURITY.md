# Security

This is **experimental, unaudited software**, published as an engineering reference. It has not been
reviewed by a third party and must not be used with real funds without an independent audit.

The design and its assumptions are documented in [`docs/THREAT_MODEL.md`](docs/THREAT_MODEL.md).

## Reporting

Found an issue? Please open a GitHub issue for non-sensitive reports, or reach the author privately
for anything exploitable rather than filing it publicly.
